Supporting education and innovation.

Prayatna is an initiative under Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya (SVVV), Indore which aims at providing quality education to the students of government schools through interactive medium. Prayatna is a Non Profitable Organization run and managed by the students and faculties of SVVV who believe that Communication Skills and Computer Education are the two important aspects of education which cannot be overlooked.
Prayatna wishes to work with the teachers at government schools to gain the valuable experience from them and boost the social serving agenda of the NPO. Also, this will inculcate in our volunteers the feeling of giving back to the society.

What we Teach

Prayatna focuses on teaching English and Computer Education through interactive and creative mediums so as to infuse the knowledge of these vital subjects in students. Additionally, प्रyatna also tries to give students artistic knowledge, general knowledge, and awareness. Main aim of प्रyatna is to give children enough idea of possible career paths they can pursue in the future, and to be able to live a bright future.
